Know Your Doctor

Dr. Poonam is a pediatrician in Basvangudi. She has 20 years of experience in this field. During her tenure, she's managed General Pediatric wards, PICU, Special clinics like Asthma clinic, and Adolescent Obesity clinics. She started Adolescent subspecialty in St Martha's. She was one of the teaching faculty for the DNB programme in St. Martha's and Co-guide to PG Research and Thesis. She is also a committee member for Infectious Diseases conference and National Adolescent conferences. At present, she's practicing at Kangaroo Clinic, Basvangudi, Bangalore. Apart from being a member of the Indian Pediatric Association and Karnataka Medical Council, Dr. Poonam is committed to providing the best healthcare treatment for children. Pediatricians are concerned with the health of infants, children, and teenagers. Dr. Poonam's approach lies in performing diagnostic tests to obtain information about patients' medical conditions and administering therapies, medications, and vaccinations to treat illnesses, disorders, and injuries. A part of being a pediatrician is not only dealing with the children but also dealing with the family. A typical day for Dr. Poonam starts by visiting the newborn nursery, addressing the concerns of the parents, and moving further to pediatric care.
